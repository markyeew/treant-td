Synergy Guide

How Synergies Work
✦ Place compatible trees adjacent to activate their synergy
✦ All 8 surrounding cells count — including diagonal
✦ Diagonal gives 50% of the bonus
✦ A tower holds multiple synergies from different neighbours
✦ Synergies update live when towers are sold
✦ Synergised towers show a golden border glow in game
Ground Synergies
🌳+🌿
Rooted Fortress
Oak gets
+20% attack range — hits enemies further up the lane before they reach your wall
Willow gets
+0.5s slow duration — enemies stay slowed significantly longer
⚡ Combined Power
Oak intercepts enemies earlier while Willow ensures they crawl through the kill zone. Together they extend your effective defensive range by nearly a full cell.
Best placed with Oak blocking the lane and Willow directly behind it
🌳+🌴
Iron Root
Both trees get
+25% ground damage — devastating against heavy ground units like Stone Golems
⚡ Combined Power
The two strongest ground towers amplify each other. A Golem that survives Oak alone won't survive Oak+Mangrove together. This pairing essentially creates a 25% damage zone for all ground enemies passing through.
Placement order doesn't matter — both get the full bonus
🌿+🌴
Bog Trap
Willow gets
+1s slow duration — aura slows enemies for much longer
Mangrove gets
+1s root duration — enemies are immobilised significantly longer on hit
⚡ Combined Power
The most powerful crowd-control combination in the game. Willow slows all enemies in its aura while Mangrove roots each one it shoots. Enemies caught here are essentially frozen while all your other towers shred them. At max upgrade, enemies can be locked in place for over 4 seconds.
Place these two adjacent in your kill zone for maximum effect
🌴+🌴
Root Network
Both Mangroves get
+20% root duration and +10% damage — overlapping splash zones create an inescapable trap
⚡ Combined Power
Two Mangroves side by side have overlapping splash zones. Any enemy entering the area takes splash from both and gets rooted repeatedly. Combined with the +20% root bonus, enemies are effectively pinned in the zone indefinitely as long as both Mangroves keep firing.
Place side by side in a narrow chokepoint for maximum splash overlap
Air Synergies
🌲+🍀
Sky Dominion
Both trees get
+20% damage vs air enemies — critical for taking down Void Drakes fast
⚡ Combined Power
Pine already deals +50% air damage. With Sky Dominion that becomes +70%. Elder also jumps from +25% to +45% air. Together they create an anti-air corridor that can one-shot Void Drakes at higher upgrades. Essential placement before Wave 17.
Place together in your back line — Elder's range covers both their positions
🌲+🌿
Frosted Needles
Pine gets
Each needle shot applies +1s slow — even the fastest Shadow Sprites and Drakes get caught
⚡ Combined Power
Pine's fast fire rate means it's constantly applying slow to multiple targets. Combined with Willow's existing aura slow, enemies in this zone are slowed from two sources simultaneously. Shadow Sprites at Wave 3 become trivially easy to handle with this combo active.
Great early-mid game pairing — available from Wave 1
🌿+🍀
Ancient Mist
Willow gets
+1 cell aura radius — covers a dramatically larger area of the grid
Elder gets
+10% boss damage — more power against the Hollow King
⚡ Combined Power
An extra cell of Willow aura at a standard cell size adds coverage equivalent to nearly a full row of the grid. Every enemy — including the Hollow King — now enters the slow zone much earlier. Elder then hits the slowed boss for bonus damage. This combo is strongest in late game boss waves.
Willow placed adjacent to Elder — Elder's attack range handles the rest
Fire Rate Synergies
🌲+🌲
Needle Storm
Both Pines get
+15% fire rate — needles fly significantly faster from both trees
⚡ Combined Power
Two Pines already fire fast. At +15% fire rate each, they collectively put out 30% more needles per second between them. Against a Shadow Sprite swarm or a Void Drake formation, this effectively doubles your anti-air DPS output. Two Pines side by side is often better than one Elder for air defence.
Place side by side in your back row — the Needle Storm corridor shreds swarms
🌲+🌴
Thorn Wall
Pine gets
+10% fire rate — shoots slightly faster
Mangrove gets
+0.3 splash radius — root burst catches more enemies in the explosion
⚡ Combined Power
A flexible mixed-threat pairing. Pine handles air and long-range targets faster while Mangrove's wider splash catches more ground enemies per shot. This combo handles both threat types simultaneously making it ideal for mixed waves where you face ground and air enemies at the same time.
Versatile — good from mid-game onwards when mixed waves begin
Power Synergies
🌳+🌲
Forest Vanguard
Oak gets
+10% max HP — more durable as a frontline blocker
Pine gets
+10% damage — stronger shots across its long range
⚡ Combined Power
The classic guard-and-sniper formation. Oak tanks the front line and lives longer, giving Pine more time to fire from behind. Oak's longer survival means enemies stay in Pine's range longer, maximising Pine's total DPS output. Simple but highly effective from Wave 1 to the end.
Oak in front, Pine one or two rows behind — the fundamental defensive formation

Oak becomes nearly unkillable in the early lanes as it slowly regenerates HP between waves, meaning you rarely need to repair or sell it. Elder meanwhile gains a flat damage bonus that applies to everything — ground, air, and boss enemies alike. This frees up sunlight you'd spend replacing damaged Oaks.
Place Oak directly adjacent to Elder — Oak protects Elder's position while healing

+0.5 cell splash radius — roots a significantly wider area on each impact

Mangrove's wider splash means it roots entire columns of enemies with a single shot. Elder then immediately fires into the rooted crowd for bonus ground damage. This combo essentially creates a fire-and-freeze cycle where Mangrove locks them and Elder demolishes them. Devastating against Golem waves.
Elder placed adjacent to Mangrove — fires into the rooted cluster immediately after
