Synergy Guide

How Synergies Work
✦ Place compatible trees adjacent to activate their synergy
✦ All 8 surrounding cells count — including diagonal
✦ Diagonal gives 50% of the bonus
✦ A tower can hold multiple synergies from different neighbours
✦ Duplicate pairs don't stack — Oak next to 2 Pines = once
✦ Synergies update live when towers are sold
✦ Synergised towers show a golden border glow in game
