✦ Synergy Rules
• Any two matching trees placed adjacent (including diagonal) trigger their synergy
• Diagonal adjacency gives 50% of the bonus
• A tower can hold multiple synergies simultaneously — one per unique neighbour type
• Duplicate pairs don't double — Oak next to 2 Pines = Forest Vanguard once
• Synergies update live — sell a tower and its bonus disappears instantly
• Synergised towers show a golden glow in game
